<blockquote class="wp-block-quote"><p>Yoga (Sanskrit: योग) is the physical, mental, and spiritual practices or disciplines which originated in ancient India with a view to attain a state of permanent peace of mind in order to experience one&#8217;s true self.</p><p>The term yoga can be derived from either of two roots, yujir yoga (to yoke) or yuj samādhau (to concentrate). The Yoga Sūtras of Patañjali defines yoga as &#8220;the stilling of the changing states of the mind&#8221; (Sanskrit: योग: चित्त-वृत्ति निरोध:). Yoga has also been popularly defined as &#8220;union with the divine&#8221; in other contexts and traditions.</p><cite><a href="https://en.wikiquote.org/wiki/Yoga">wikiquote / Yoga</a></cite></blockquote>
