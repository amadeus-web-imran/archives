# Non Violent Communication

An exercize I did (in Feb 2019) keeping in mind a dear friend I had quarreled with a year before, this lovely exercise taught by a [lovely lady](https://www.linkedin.com/in/vedvyasinnerspace31) left me in tears.

It teaches us to identify our painful and joyful needs and feelings and to consider a person we are upset with or having trouble getting to understand as a part of us.

For more details, please write to [Shyleswari](mailto:shylahrd@gmail.com) or visit [VVIS](https://www.vedvyasinnerspace.com/).

Imran, Mar 2022.
