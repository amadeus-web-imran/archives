In <a href="../sri-aurobindo/">this section dedicated to Sri Aurobindo</a>, we have republished <a href="https://www.sriaurobindoashram.org/sriaurobindo/writings.php" target="_blank">his complete works</a> below, since his works became open domain in 2010.

For a <a href="https://legacy.yieldmore.org/people/sri-aurobindo/bio/" target="_blank">biography, see here</a>. We had earlier published 4 of his works viz: <a href="https://legacy.yieldmore.org/works/essays-on-the-gita/" target="_blank">Essays on the Gita</a>, <a href="https://legacy.yieldmore.org/works/hour-of-god/" target="_blank">Hour of God</a>, <a href="https://legacy.yieldmore.org/works/problem-of-rebirth/" target="_blank">The Problem of Rebirth</a> and <a href="https://legacy.yieldmore.org/works/savitri/" target="_blank">Savitri</a>. Some of his quotes on Hinduism, you will find on <a href="https://legacy.yieldmore.org/topics/religion/" target="_blank">our legacy religion page</a>.

<a href="https://imran.yieldmore.org/" target="_blank">Imran</a> is a volunteer at <a href="https://sriaurobindodhama.org/" target="_blank">Sri Aurobindo Dhama</a> and has been published at <a href="https://journal.aurobharati.in/author/imran-ali-namazi/" target="_blank">Renaissance (an AuroBharati publication)</a>. Imran has <a href="https://imran.yieldmore.org/impelled/for/msa/" target="_blank">dedicated a few poems to Mother/Sri Aurobindo</a> and used to distribute copies of <a href="../sri-aurobindos-gita/">Sri Aurobindo's Gita</a>.

Some other sites that may be of interest to the Newcomer / Aurobindonian are: <a href="http://beyondman.org/" target="_blank">Beyond Man</a>, <a href="https://auromere.wordpress.com/" target="_blank">AuroMere</a> and <a href="https://aurobindo.ru/" target="_blank">Aurobindo.ru</a>.
