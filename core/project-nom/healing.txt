# Healing Practices

## Aromatherapy

Aromatherapy is a ** holistic healing treatment ** that uses natural plant extracts to promote health and well-being. Sometimes it's called essential oil therapy.

## Bach Flower Remedies

Bach flower remedies are an alternative or complementary treatment that is used for emotional problems and pain. They're made out of watered-down extracts from the flowers of wild plants.

## Homoeopathy

Homoeopathy is a medical system based on the belief that the body can cure itself.Tiny amounts of natural substances like plants and minerals are used to stimulate the healing process.

## Mudras

These are symbolic gestures practiced with hands and fingers that facilitate the flow of energy in the subtle body,gestures created to facilitate the flow of energy,prana in the body and mind for optimal health.

## Pranic Healing

This is a highly developed and tested system of energy treatment that uses prana (life energy) to balance, harmonize and  transform the body's energy processes.

## Reiki

This is an energy healing technique that uses gentle hand movements by a Reiki master to guide the flow of healthy energy through the body to reduce stress and promote healing.

## Siddha

Prescribed by Shyam to Imran a few years ago, Siddha is a South Indian form of medicine, based on plants, trees and herbs, said to be devised and practiced by Siddhars over the ages.
